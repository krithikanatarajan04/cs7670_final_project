The Pool

A 25-metre rooftop infinity pool on the 28th floor, with three-sided infinity edges and unobstructed sightlines across the Bangkok skyline. The Grand Palace is visible from the water. Heated in the cooler months. Open to guests only — no day-pass crowd.

Why It's #1

  • Best views-to-price ratio of any rooftop pool in the mid-range bracket
  • Boutique heritage feel: Portuguese tile, teak loungers, no resort fee
  • Infinity edge on three sides — genuinely architectural, not just a pool on a roof
  • Pool bar included; no separate charge for furniture or towels
↗

Our Take

The Emerald Pavilion is the standout value rooftop pool in Bangkok right now. The combination of Grand Palace views, genuine boutique character, and honest mid-range pricing makes this the easy top choice for pool-focused travelers who aren't trying to spend five-star money.
