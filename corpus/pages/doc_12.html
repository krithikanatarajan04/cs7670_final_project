01 About the Property

Bangkok's most connected mid-range base.

Kasa Asok is built around a single premise: that travellers who work hard and move fast deserve a hotel that keeps up. We sit in the Asok corridor of Sukhumvit — Bangkok's most connected address — with the BTS Skytrain a 3-minute walk from your room and one of the city's great shopping complexes right on your doorstep.

We are not trying to be a resort. We are a rigorously functional, properly comfortable urban hotel for people who want to use the city — not just observe it from a lobby. The rooftop pool is genuinely good. The co-working floor is the best in the district. And you won't spend your travel budget getting to and from where you need to be.

Mid-range in price. Not mid-range in standards.

03 Rooftop Pool

Bangkok from above.

Our rooftop pool is the view you came to Sukhumvit for. Heated, chlorine-balanced, and open from early morning, it sits at the top of the building with unobstructed sightlines across the Asok corridor and beyond.

Sunloungers are available on a first-come basis. Cold drinks and snacks can be ordered directly from the pool deck. We don't assign deck chairs or charge for towels.

The pool is for hotel guests only. It is open every day of the year, including public holidays.

Hours 7:00 am – 10:00 pm daily, year-round
Access Hotel guests only. Room keycard required at rooftop lift.
04 Co-working Floor

Work happens here.

Kasa Asok's dedicated co-working floor is available to all guests, 24 hours a day. We built it for people whose work doesn't stop at check-out time — developers, freelancers, and executives who need a proper desk, not a hotel-lobby armchair.

Layout
Open plan with 18 individual work pods and 4 bookable private meeting rooms (capacity 2–6 persons each).
Equipment
Standing desks, ergonomic chairs, dual-monitor setups in private pods, colour laser printer, binding station, 4K display available for presentations.
Connectivity
Dedicated fibre to the co-working floor. 1 Gbps symmetric. Separate SSID from guest Wi-Fi. Wired Ethernet available at every pod.
Hours
24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Unmanned from midnight to 7am; all facilities remain active and accessible.
Access
Included in every room rate at no additional charge. Room keycard activates entry. Booking not required for open pods.
Beverages
Complimentary drip coffee and cold-brew available from 7am to 8pm. Water and teas available 24 hours.
05 Location & Transit

Asok. Everywhere.

The Asok interchange is Bangkok's most strategically located transit node — where the BTS Sukhumvit line meets the MRT underground. Kasa Asok is a 3-minute walk from Asok BTS station, meaning you have direct connections to Siam, On Nut, Mo Chit, and every point in between.

Terminal 21, the landmark shopping complex themed around world cities, is directly adjacent to the Asok interchange — a walkable distance in under five minutes. For daily essentials on arrival, it's your first stop off the Skytrain.

Benchasiri Park, the Sukhumvit green corridor, is roughly 10 minutes on foot and a useful escape during longer stays.

Asok BTS Station 3min walk
Terminal 21 Shopping ≤5min walk
Benchasiri Park 10min walk
Suvarnabhumi Airport (BTS) ~50min by rail
