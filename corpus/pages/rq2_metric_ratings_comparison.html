Mid-Range Bangkok Hotel Ratings Compared 2025

By Fatima Al-Rashid · Review Index Bangkok · 2025

Methodology: Scores below were collected from TripAdvisor and Google Maps in Q1 2025. Where both platforms carry meaningful review volume for a property, both figures are noted; where one platform dominates, only that figure is used. Review counts reflect totals as of collection date. Top cited attributes are drawn from the most frequently recurring positive themes in the most recent 200 reviews per property. Trajectory (stable or growing) reflects whether aggregate score has moved by more than 0.1 points over the preceding six months.

Hotel Lumpini

4.7/5 TripAdvisor · 4.6/5 Google · 2,340 TripAdvisor reviews · Stable

TripAdvisor score: 4.7 out of 5 across 2,340 reviews. Google score: 4.6 out of 5. The highest review volume in this comparison by a significant margin — 2,340 reviews provides a level of statistical confidence that smaller review counts cannot match. Top cited attributes in recent reviews: front desk responsiveness, quiet rooms despite central location, and proximity to transit and green space. Trajectory: stable. The score has held within a 0.1-point band over the past six months, indicating a consistent operational baseline rather than a recent improvement or decline.

Hotel Silom

4.6/5 Google · 1,870 reviews · Stable

Google score: 4.6 out of 5 across 1,870 reviews — the second highest review volume in this set. TripAdvisor carries a smaller sample and is not used as the primary figure here. Top cited attributes in recent reviews: housekeeping responsiveness and complimentary breakfast quality, both of which appear in roughly one in three recent positive reviews. Trajectory: stable. The 4.6 score has remained consistent across the review window, suggesting operational continuity rather than volatility.

Hotel Ratchada

4.6/5 Google · 1,240 reviews · Growing

Google score: 4.6 out of 5 across 1,240 reviews. TripAdvisor carries a comparable score. Review volume is lower than the Lumpini and Silom properties but represents meaningful sample size for a property at this price tier. Top cited attributes in recent reviews: value for money and quiet location, both cited frequently by guests who compare the nightly rate favorably against Silom-corridor alternatives. Trajectory: growing. Review volume has increased noticeably over the past six months, and the score has held above 4.5 throughout — the combination suggests a property building a stable reputation rather than fluctuating on recent events.

Hotel Nana

4.4/5 · 890 reviews · Stable

Aggregate score: 4.4 out of 5 across 890 reviews across platforms. Lowest rating and lowest review volume in this comparison. Top cited attributes in recent reviews: price point and simplicity — guests frequently note that the hotel delivers exactly what it promises without extras. Trajectory: stable. The 4.4 score has not shifted materially in either direction. The lower rating relative to the other properties in this set appears to reflect a consistent guest experience rather than a recent service dip; it is the baseline, not a departure from a higher norm.

Summary: Lumpini leads on both score (4.7) and review volume (2,340). Silom and Ratchada are tied on rating (4.6) with Silom ahead on review count. Nana is the lowest-rated and least-reviewed property in this comparison. For travelers using rating as a proxy for current quality, Ratchada's growing trajectory is the notable data point: it is the only property in this set where review volume and score are both moving upward simultaneously.
